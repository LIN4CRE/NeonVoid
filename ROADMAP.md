# 🗺️ NEON VOID | Roadmap to v2.0

The journey through the void has just begun. Version 2.0 will transition **NEON VOID** from a survival arcade game into a full-fledged space odyssey with deeper systems, more variety, and a more immersive world.

---

## 🚀 Phase 1: Content Expansion (The "Void Reach" Update)
*Focus: Adding variety to the gameplay loop and increasing the number of challenges.*

### 👾 New Enemy Archetypes
- **The Hive Mind**: A massive carrier that spawns smaller drones.
- **Siphoners**: Enemies that steal your XP/Energy to heal themselves.
- **Phase-Shifters**: Enemies that become invisible or intangible periodically.
- **Mirror-Ships**: Mimics that copy the player's current weapon configuration.

### 🌌 Environmental Hazards
- **Black Holes**: Periodic gravitational anomalies that pull everything toward a center point.
- **Ion Storms**: Random zones that disable certain abilities (e.g., Dash) or scramble controls.
- **Nebula Fog**: Areas with reduced visibility, forcing players to rely on radar/proximity alerts.

### 🏆 New Bosses
- **The Singularity Core**: A boss that manipulates the map, flipping gravity or shrinking the play area.
- **Neon Hydra**: A multi-headed boss where each head has a different attack pattern.

---

## 🛠️ Phase 2: System Depth (The "Core Evolution" Update)
*Focus: Enhancing the RPG elements and strategic build-crafting.*

### 🧬 Advanced Upgrade Tree
- **Synergies**: Combining specific upgrades (e.g., Singularity Shot + Multi-Shot) unlocks a "Hyper-Ability."
- **Equipment Slots**: Introduction of "Cores" and "Plating" that can be swapped between runs.
- **Active Abilities**: Move beyond passive buffs to active skills (e.g., a temporary time-slow "Chronos" field).

### 📈 Progression & Rewards
- **Achievement System**: Unlock special ship skins and titles by completing challenges.
- **Unlockable Ships**: Different starting ships with unique base stats (e.g., The Tank, The Scout, The Glass Cannon).
- **Daily Missions**: Rotating challenges for bonus Data Scraps.

---

## ✨ Phase 3: Visual & Audio Overhaul (The "Lumen" Update)
*Focus: Pushing the aesthetic to a professional, AAA-indie standard.*

### 🎨 Visual Fidelity
- **Dynamic Lighting**: Implement a more advanced lighting engine with real-time shadows and bloom.
- **Sprite-Based Entities**: Transition from purely procedural shapes to high-quality neon sprites for more detail.
- **Screen-Space Effects**: Add chromatic aberration and motion blur for high-speed dashes.

### 🎹 Auditory Evolution
- **Adaptive OST**: A fully dynamic soundtrack that blends layers based on the player's current combo and health.
- **Voice-Over**: System alerts and Boss introductions using synthesized futuristic voices.

---

## 🌐 Phase 4: Social & Ecosystem (The "Galactic Network" Update)
*Focus: Community engagement and long-term replayability.*

### 👥 Competitive Play
- **Global Leaderboards**: Compete for the highest score/wave across the world.
- **Ghost Runs**: Race against the recording of your best run or a friend's run.
- **Challenge Seeds**: Share a specific "Void Seed" so other players can fight the exact same wave sequence.

### 🛠️ Modding Support
- **Custom Upgrades**: Allow users to define their own upgrade effects via a simple JSON config.
- **Custom Skins**: Support for user-created ship textures.

---

## 📅 Estimated Timeline
- **v1.1 - v1.5**: Iterative balance and Phase 1 content.
- **v1.6 - v1.9**: Implementation of Phase 2 systems.
- **v2.0**: The Grand Convergence (All phases merged into one massive update).
