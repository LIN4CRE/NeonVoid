# 📝 NEON VOID v2.0.0 Release Notes

## 🚀 Major Milestone: The Singularity Update

Version 2.0.0 transforms **NEON VOID** from a survival arcade game into a strategic rogue-lite experience. This update focuses on depth, polish, and professional delivery.

### 🆕 New Features

#### ⚔️ Combat & Abilities
- **Active Ability: Chronos Field**: Press `E` to slow down time for 3 seconds, allowing for precision dodging and attacking.
- **Active Ability: Void Pulse**: Press `Q` to release a radial shockwave that damages and pushes back all nearby enemies.
- **Synergy System**: Unlocking specific combinations of upgrades now grants "Hyper-Abilities":
    - **Hyper Vortex**: Projectiles now exert a powerful gravitational pull.
    - **Hyper Scythe**: Massive damage boost to piercing bullets.
    - **Hyper Sustain**: Doubled health regeneration.

#### 👹 Enemies & Bosses
- **The Singularity Core**: A new, massive boss that uses Event Horizons and Repulsor Waves to manipulate the battlefield.
- **New Elites**: Added Hive ships, Siphoners, and Phase-Shifters to increase wave variety.
- **Environmental Hazards**: Black Holes now spawn procedurally, affecting both players and enemies.

#### 🎨 Visuals & Audio
- **Dynamic Backgrounds**: Added procedural Nebulae and Stardust for a cinematic feel.
- **Adaptive OST**: Music now shifts tempo and tone during boss fights.
- **Polish**: Enhanced particle effects, screen shake, and high-contrast bloom filters.

#### 📱 Mobile UX
- **Action Suite**: Dedicated on-screen buttons for Dash, Chronos Field, and Pause.
- **Optimized Inputs**: Improved touch responsiveness and prevented default browser behaviors.

### 🛠️ Improvements & Fixes
- **Collision Optimization**: Reduced complexity of collision checks for better performance on mobile.
- **Balance Pass**: Adjusted enemy health and spawn rates for a smoother difficulty curve.
- **Bug Fixes**: Resolved issues with XP gain and synergy triggering.

### 📈 Roadmap Preview
Looking ahead to v2.1 and beyond:
- Global Leaderboards.
- Unlockable Ships with unique base stats.
- Advanced modding support for custom upgrades.
