**NEON VOID v2.0** is a high-octane, rogue-lite space survival game. Navigate through a neon-infused void, fight off waves of galactic entities, salvage data scraps, and upgrade your ship to become the ultimate survivor.

Experience a blend of strategic build-crafting and twitch-reflex combat in a stunning retro-futuristic aesthetic.

## 🚀 v2.0 New Features

### 🛡️ Advanced Combat
- **Active Abilities**: Use the `Chronos Field` (Time Slow) and `Void Pulse` (Radial Knockback) to turn the tide of battle.
- **Synergy System**: Combine specific upgrades to unlock "Hyper-Abilities" like **Hyper Vortex** and **Hyper Scythe**.
- **Complex Bosses**: Encounter the **Singularity Core**, a boss that manipulates gravity and space-time.

### 👾 New Threats
- **Elite Entities**: Fight Hive ships, Siphoners, and Phase-Shifters.
- **Dynamic Hazards**: Navigate around procedural Black Holes that pull everything into their event horizon.
- **Adaptive Scaling**: Enemies evolve their shields and health based on your progress.

### 🎨 Visual & Audio Overhaul
- **Atmospheric Depth**: Cinematic nebulae and dynamic stardust backgrounds.
- **Adaptive Soundtrack**: Music that shifts intensity based on combat state (Normal $\rightarrow$ Danger $\rightarrow$ Boss).
- **Enhanced Feedback**: Polished screen-shake, damage numbers, and high-contrast neon effects.

## 🕹️ How to Play

### Controls
| Action | Desktop | Mobile |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Move** | `WASD` / `Arrow Keys` | Virtual Joystick |
| **Aim** | Mouse Cursor | Automatic |
| **Dash** | `Space` / `Shift` | DASH Button |
| **Chronos Field** | `E` | TIME Button |
| **Void Pulse** | `Q` | N/A (coming soon) |
| **Pause** | `Esc` | II Button |

### Goal
Survive as many waves as possible. Collect XP to level up and salvage data scraps upon death to buy permanent upgrades in the hangar.
